Here are some facts about habits!

Fact #1

Over 40% of what you do is habitual. Take charge of nearly half your day by setting good habits! It would be a shame to have just under half a day wasted regularly.

Fact #2

It takes 66 days on average to create a habit. Consistency is key! Don't get discouraged if you struggle when changing your lifestyle!

Fact #3

Habits can remind us of what our goals used to be!

Fact #4

Habits that involve some sort of physical movement are easier to create and maintain!
